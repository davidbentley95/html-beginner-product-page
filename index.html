Our Story

In the heart of Vancouver, BC, two best friends, Bavid and Dranko, embarked on a revolutionary journey to transform the fitness and nutrition landscape. United by their passion for health and their extensive research on optimizing heart rate to enhance workout and nutrition outcomes, they founded a start-up that stands at the intersection of science and wellness.

Their pioneering work, which has been widely published and celebrated in the fitness community, focuses on tailoring fitness routines and dietary plans to individual heart rate responses, ensuring that each person they touch is on the most efficient path to achieving their health goals. This commitment to personalized fitness and nutrition has quickly propelled their company to the forefront of the industry, making a profound impact on the lives of those looking to unlock their full physical potential.
